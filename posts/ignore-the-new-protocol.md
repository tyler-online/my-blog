---
title: "Ignore the new protocol"
date: "May 15, 2026"
---

People love talking about the new thing they’re doing. They get excited and start sharing their new protocol 2 weeks in (a supplement, sleep schedule, project, etc). I think you should ignore those. Instead, I want to know about the protocol you’re still finding effective 6 or 12 months in, after the initial excitement has faded.
